Welcome to FlyerConnect!

FlyerConnect is a web tool to make the sometimes mundane activity of flying much more social. Connect with Facebook friends, discover coworkers on your flights, and share your activity on Twitter.

Before you can continue, we must authenticate your account with Facebook.
